About us

Building the bridge between talent and opportunity

Rojgar Setu started as a project with one goal: make it simple for job seekers across India to find genuine openings, and just as simple for small businesses to find the right person fast.

No subscriptions for employers, no fees for job seekers, and no clutter — just a direct line between the two sides of the hiring process.

✓
Free for job seekers

Search, apply and track every application without paying anything.

⚡
Fast for employers

Publish a listing in under two minutes and start receiving applications the same day.

📍
Built for every city

From metros to smaller towns, openings are organised by location and category.
